Chicken Karage

Description:

Ever had the most succulent,mouth-watering fried chicken. Well you haven't until you tried this karage recipe. Imagine meeting God at the have to imagine if you eat this chicken.

Ingredients:

Yields 4 Servings

  • 1 lb skin-on chicken thigh, cubed
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 egg, beaten
  • 1/3 cup potato starch

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, sake, sugar, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. Mix well then let set for 15 minutes in the fridge.
  2. Add egg and potato starch to the chicken. Mix Well
  3. Heat oil to 340 F and fry chicken till golden brown (5-7 min). Enjoy!
